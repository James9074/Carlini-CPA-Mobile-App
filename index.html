Services

    
        

Tax Preparation

Carlini CPA offers accounting services including individual and business tax return preparation. We will take the time to understand your situation to provide personalized and timely services that you and your business deserve.

    
    
        

Audit, Review, & Compilation

If your business or your organization requires an audited, reviewed, or compiled financial statement, it is very important to understand the requirements of the financial statements so that the proper level of service can be completed.

    
    

Bookkeeping

Do you need help with your accounting? We have over 40 years of accounting services experience in a wide variety of industries using many different accounting packages and systems.

    
    

QuickBooks

Do you still use an excel spreadsheet to do your accounting? We can setup a QuickBooks file and either provide the accounting services for you or can train on its use and provide a monthly or quarterly monitoring role. Maybe you are already using QuickBooks but you want to get more out of it.

    
    

Payroll

At Carlini CPA we offer an entire suite of payroll services. We have e-file capabilities to e-file state and federal monthly, quarterly, and annual payroll reports. Let us save you time and hassle by preparing your required payroll filings.

    
    

IRS Resolution

As Certified Public Accountants, we can represent you before the IRS. Dealing with the IRS on either a personal or business level can be intimidating and stressful. If you have received a tax notice, bring it to us and let us devise a plan and strategy for resolution.

    
    

Management Services

Are you getting the most from your supply chain? We can perform a review of your high dollar supplier purchases to determine if savings can be realized. We have extensive experience dealing with multi-million dollar international companies and procurement contracts.
